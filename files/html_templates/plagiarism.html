This is a bidirectional graph showing how are web domains taking parts of articles from articles on other web domains. We consider an article to be a plagiate, if 25% of it is taken from another article. If domain with color red has taken an article from domain with color blue, there will be a stronger link between them with the blue color. Links in graph based on words represent how many words are taken between web domains in plagiate articles. Graphs are generated for all time articles and for articles from last two weeks.
